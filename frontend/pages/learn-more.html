First. Make an Account. If you already haven't.

If you have an account already please just Login. For those who don't. Please click Sign up to make an account. You can also find this in the Login Page. You can access it by clicking the dropdown menu on the top right corner. If you don't see it on the top screen.

Second. Now You Can Submit a Memorial.

Go click the Create Memorial on the top screen. If you don't see that. Please click the dropdown menu on the top right corner. There you can go and click Create Memorial to submit your loved ones photo and information. You can also access that page by clicking it here.

Third. After you Submit a Request. Please Wait.

We will approve request after we check it manually. To see your requested memorial. If you don't see dashboard on the top screen. Please click the dropdown menu on the top right corner. When you have please go and click Dashboard. You can also access that page by clicking it here. In the dashboard you can see what you requested, drafts of requests you have not finished, and memorials that have been approved. You can also edit and remove the memorials.
